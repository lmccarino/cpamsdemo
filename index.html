The Lingap Program is an initiative of the Davao City Government which provides medical and hospitalization assistance to Davao City residents who are determined indigents and needs immediate medical help. The program's assistance is coursed through the government-owned Southern Philippines Medical Center (SPMC). Assistance are also extended residents who specially need financial help through medicine or hospital fee subsidies even if they are confined in private hospitals. Additionally, the program also offers funeral assistance through pauper's burial through the city's public cemetery. The program is extended to the beneficiaries through processing centers, at the Malasakit Center and satellite offices.
